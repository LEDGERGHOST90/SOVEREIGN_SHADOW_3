# Silicon Valley Bank: 5 Crypto Predictions for 2026

**Source:** Silicon Valley Bank (First Citizens Bank)
**Authors:** Anthony Vassallo (SVP Crypto), Josh Pherigo (Principal Researcher)
**Published:** December 23, 2025
**URL:** https://www.svb.com/industry-insights/fintech/2026-crypto-outlook/

## Key Predictions Summary

### 1. Institutional Capital Goes Vertical
- VC investment in US crypto companies rebounded to **$7.9 billion** in 2025 (up 44% from 2024)
- Deal volume fell 33% but median check size climbed **1.5x to $5 million**
- Seed company median valuation: **$34 million** (up 70% from 2023)
- **172 publicly traded companies** held Bitcoin in Q3 2025 (up 40% QoQ)
- Corporate holdings: ~**1 million BTC** (~5% of circulating supply)
- Major banks entering crypto: JPMorgan, Morgan Stanley, PNC, SoFi, US Bank

### 2. M&A Posts Another Banner Year
- **140+ VC-backed crypto companies** acquired in 4 quarters ending Q3 2025 (59% YoY increase)
- Major deals: Coinbase bought Deribit for **$2.9 billion**, Kraken bought NinjaTrader for **$1.5 billion**
- **18 new OCC charter applications** in 2025 (vs 1 last year)
- OCC granted conditional approval to: BitGo, Circle, Fidelity Digital Assets, Paxos, Ripple
- Ripple valued at **$40 billion** after acquisitions

### 3. Stablecoins Become "The Internet's Dollar"
- GENIUS Act (July 2025) established federal stablecoin standards
- VC investment in stablecoin companies: **$1.5 billion** in 2025 (vs <$50M in 2019)
- Global stablecoin supply expanding as banks issue tokens
- JPMorgan extended JPM Coin to public blockchains (Nov 2025)
- Bank consortium (PNC, Citi, Wells Fargo) exploring joint stablecoin via Early Warning Services

### 4. Real World Asset (RWA) Tokenization Goes Mainstream
- On-chain RWAs crossed **$36 billion** in 2025
- BlackRock's BUIDL fund surpassed **$500 million**
- Franklin Templeton tokenized funds: **$400+ million**
- Polymarket: **$3.7 billion** monthly volume, **$8 billion** valuation
- Kalshi: **$11 billion** valuation (Dec 2025)

### 5. AI and Crypto Redefine Digital Commerce
- For every VC dollar in crypto, **40 cents** went to companies also building AI (vs 18 cents last year)
- AI wallets moving from prototypes to pilots
- DePIN networks (Akash, io.net) attracting AI compute workloads
- Blockchain solving AI trust problem through provenance protocols

## SVB's Strategic Outlook
"Treat crypto as infrastructure in 2026. Build or partner now around stablecoin settlement, custody/compliance rails and tokenized-asset distribution. The winners will be the platforms that make these capabilities invisible, regulated and usable at scale."
