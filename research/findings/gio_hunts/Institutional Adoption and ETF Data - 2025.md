## Key Policy Developments

### U.S. Regulatory Milestones
1. **SAB 121 Repeal (Jan 23, 2025):** Replaced with SAB 122, allowing banks to treat digital assets like any other asset
2. **Strategic Bitcoin Reserve (March 2025):** Executive Order designating 200,000+ seized BTC as national asset
3. **GENIUS Act (July 2025):** Codified how banks and custodians handle stablecoins and digital assets

### Global Regulatory Progress
- **Europe:** MiCA regulation took full effect
- **Dubai:** VARA granted full-scope licenses
- **Singapore:** MAS granted comprehensive licenses
