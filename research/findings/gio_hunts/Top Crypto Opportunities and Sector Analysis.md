# Top Crypto Opportunities and Sector Analysis

## Tiger Research: 10 Crypto Market Shifts for 2026

**Source:** Tiger Research
**Authors:** Daniel Kim, Ryan Yoon, Jay Jo, Ekko An
**Published:** December 26, 2025
**URL:** https://reports.tiger-research.com/p/2026-crypto-market-outlook-eng

### Key Thesis
The crypto industry is joining the mainstream. Institutions have become key market players. Capital moves to projects that generate real revenue. Short-term price fluctuations no longer matter. Sustainable business models have become essential.

### 10 Major Market Shifts for 2026

**1. Institutional Capital Stays in Bitcoin**
As institutions lead the market, capital flows have become cautious. These investors avoid unproven assets and limit scope to Bitcoin and Ethereum. Market growth will concentrate solely on assets meeting institutional standards.

**2. Revenue-Generating Projects Dominate**
Price drops in 85% of new tokens post-TGE expose limits of narrative-driven growth. Hype-based projects will be replaced at increasing rate. Market shifts toward projects with real revenue and sound fundamentals.

**3. Direct Value Return Tokenomics**
Utility-focused tokenomics failed. Governance voting rights didn't attract investors. Market now demands clear value returns through:
- Buybacks and burns
- Structures where protocol growth immediately impacts token price
- New innovative models emerging from this shift

**4. M&A Acceleration**
Web3 is maturing. Competition for market dominance intensifies. M&A is fastest way to scale and strengthen competitiveness. Winners will drive aggressive M&A activity.

**5. Robotics Data Collection via Blockchain**
Robotics industry growing. Real-world data for robot training critical. Blockchain-based decentralized crowdsourcing solves data collection problem. New gig economy centered on robotics will emerge.

**6. Prediction Markets in Media**
Traditional media firms will adopt prediction markets as survival strategy. Readers transition from passive consumption to active participation by staking capital on news outcomes.

**7. Traditional Finance Builds Own Chains for RWA**
Traditional financial institutions are primary suppliers for RWA market. These firms will likely build own chains to maintain market leadership. RWA projects lacking independent asset supply will lose competitive edge.

**8. BTCFi Growth**
Launch of ETH stake ETFs will push BTC ETF holders to seek yield. BTCFi fills this gap. As large funds enter BTC, need for asset utility will rise.

**9. Fintech Apps Lead Crypto Adoption**
As rules clear up, fintech apps become top choice for crypto trade. New users no longer need crypto exchanges. Next wave of growth led by fintech tools.

**10. Privacy Tech for Institutional Adoption**
Chain transparency reveals trade plans - weakness for large firms. Privacy tech is vital tool for institutions to join market. Big capital will only flow in if trade data is secure.

### Three Key DeFi Trends for 2026

**1. Unified Stablecoin Liquidity Layers**
Stablecoins in circulation exceeded $300 billion in 2025. Major hurdle: liquidity fragmentation across trading venues, blockchains, and exchanges.

Solutions emerging:
- Circle's Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol (CCTP)
- Tether's USDT0 omnichain stablecoin
- Expected result: More capital efficient, cheaper, predictable transfers

**2. DEXs Rival Centralized Exchanges**
- DEXs now account for 21%+ of all crypto trading (highest ever)
- Improved UX, intents-based trading, dark AMM models on Solana
- Prediction: DEXs could hit 50% of all crypto trading by end of 2026

Key drivers:
- Coinbase data breach (May 2025)
- Binance $283M refund after unfair trade closures (Oct 2025)
- General CEX technical issues and account restrictions

**3. Privacy Push Fuels Adoption**
- Zcash rallied 860% in Q4 2025, hitting $711 (highest since 2016)
- Ethereum Foundation expanding privacy infrastructure
- Privacy is prerequisite for institutional adoption

Institutional need:
- Protect pricing, strategy, sensitive investment positions
- Private multi-signature wallets required
- Canton Network building institutional-grade privacy
