# Fidelity Digital Assets: 2024 Bitcoin Halving - One Year Later

**Source:** Fidelity Digital Assets, May 22, 2025
**Author:** Daniel Gray, Senior Research Analyst
**URL:** https://www.fidelitydigitalassets.com/research-and-insights/2024-bitcoin-halving-one-year-later

## Executive Summary

Bitcoin presents a nuanced narrative one year after its fourth halving (April 19, 2024), with signs pointing toward consolidation, network resilience, and growing institutional adoption. Returns have been more measured compared to previous cycles, but structural metrics suggest a strengthening foundation.

## Current Cycle Performance (as of April 2025)

At block height 892,500 (25% into fifth halving epoch):

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Price at Halving (Apr 19, 2024) | $63,762 |
| Price (Apr 15, 2025) | $83,671 |
| Return from Halving | +31% |
| Recent Price Range | $82,000-$85,000 |
| Price Recovery | Back above $100,000 |

## Historical Cycle Comparison

| Epoch | Return at Same Point |
|-------|---------------------|
| Third Epoch | +300% |
| Fourth Epoch | +567% (from $8,591 to $57,341) |
| Fifth Epoch (Current) | +31% |

**Key Insight:** This cycle is unfolding more cautiously than historical precedent would suggest. Bitcoin's more muted returns likely reflect a maturing market digesting extrinsic tailwinds and headwinds.

## Bitcoin Market Dominance

Bitcoin dominance (excluding stablecoins) has reached **72.4%** as of May 11, 2025 - an eight-year high since March 2017.

**Dominance Changes Since Fourth Halving:**
- Bitcoin: +13% (64% → 72%)
- Ethereum: -56%
- Solana: -25%
- All Other Crypto: +67% (9% → 15%)

**Interpretation:** Greater fragmentation but no clear alternative leader emerging.

## Mining Network Health

**Hash Rate Milestones:**
- Daily hash rate exceeded 1 zettahash/second (1,000 EH/s) twice in April 2025
- 30-day mean hash rate: +40% since April 2024
- Network difficulty: +40% since April 2024

**Hash Price Dynamics:**
- Hash price (expected return per hash): -60% since April 2024
- Block reward reduced from 6.25 to 3.125 BTC
- Despite price pressure, miners maintain long-term confidence

## Key Conclusions

1. **Muted Returns ≠ Weak Fundamentals:** Price performance muted vs. past cycles, but fundamentals appear stronger than ever

2. **Market Maturation:** As Bitcoin matures, dramatic price changes become less likely due to market saturation

3. **Institutional Recognition:** Growing recognition from both institutional investors and nation states

4. **Decoupling Moment:** Recent events suggest Bitcoin may be decoupling from other assets

5. **Portfolio Role Redefinition:** This cycle may redefine Bitcoin's role in modern portfolios

## Implications for Trading

- Expect lower volatility and more measured returns compared to historical cycles
- Bitcoin dominance trend favors BTC over altcoins
- Hash rate growth signals long-term network confidence despite short-term price weakness
- Institutional adoption providing structural support floor
