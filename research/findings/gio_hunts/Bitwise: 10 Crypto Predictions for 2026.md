# Bitwise: 10 Crypto Predictions for 2026

**Source:** Bitwise Asset Management
**Authors:** Matt Hougan (Chief Investment Officer), Ryan Rasmussen (Head of Research)
**Published:** December 15, 2025
**URL:** https://bitwiseinvestments.com/crypto-market-insights/the-year-ahead-10-crypto-predictions-for-2026

## Context
2025 was a year to remember for crypto—both the highs and the lows. Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and XRP each hit all-time highs. On the downside: Crypto majors retreated sharply from their highs to end the year.

Bitwise believes 2026 will belong to the bulls. From institutional adoption to regulatory progress, crypto's prevailing positive trends are too strong to be subdued for long.

## 10 Predictions for 2026

1. **Bitcoin will break the four-year cycle and set new all-time highs**
   - Historical pattern of 4-year cycles may be broken
   - Institutional demand driving sustained growth

2. **Bitcoin will be less volatile than Nvidia**
   - Maturing asset class with institutional participation
   - Reduced volatility as market cap grows

3. **ETFs will purchase more than 100% of the new supply of Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana**
   - Institutional demand accelerating
   - Supply/demand dynamics increasingly favorable

4. **Crypto equities will outperform tech equities**
   - Companies like Coinbase, MicroStrategy expected to outperform
   - Crypto-native companies benefiting from adoption

5. **Polymarket open interest will set a new all-time high, surpassing 2024 election levels**
   - Prediction markets gaining mainstream traction
   - Regulatory clarity enabling growth

6. **Stablecoins will be blamed for destabilizing an emerging market currency**
   - Growing stablecoin adoption in developing economies
   - Potential currency substitution effects

7. **Onchain vaults—aka "ETFs 2.0"—will double in AUM**
   - DeFi yield products gaining institutional interest
   - Tokenized investment vehicles expanding

8. **Ethereum and Solana will set new all-time highs (if the CLARITY Act passes)**
   - Regulatory clarity key catalyst
   - Smart contract platforms poised for growth

9. **Half of Ivy League endowments will invest in crypto**
   - Institutional adoption expanding to educational endowments
   - Following early movers like Yale

10. **More than 100 crypto-linked ETFs will launch in the U.S.**
    - Expanding product offerings
    - Solana, XRP, and other asset ETFs expected
