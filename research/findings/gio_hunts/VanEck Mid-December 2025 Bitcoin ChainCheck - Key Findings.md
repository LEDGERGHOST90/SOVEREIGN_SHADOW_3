# VanEck Mid-December 2025 Bitcoin ChainCheck - Key Findings

**Source:** VanEck, December 22, 2025
**Authors:** Patrick Bush (Senior Investment Analyst), Matthew Sigel (Head of Digital Assets Research)
**URL:** https://www.vaneck.com/us/en/blogs/digital-assets/matthew-sigel-vaneck-mid-december-2025-bitcoin-chaincheck/

## GEO Framework Scores (as of Dec 2025)
- **Global Liquidity:** 7 (Mixed but skews bullish)
- **Ecosystem Leverage:** 7 (Low speculative energy)
- **Onchain Activity:** 3 (Very weak)

## Key Market Metrics
- Bitcoin price fell **-9%** in past 30 days
- 30-day volatility reached **>45** (highest since April 2025)
- BTC low around **$80,700** on November 22, 2025
- 30-day RSI bottomed at **~32**
- Perpetual futures basis fell to **5% annualized** (vs year avg 7.4%)
- Hash rate dropped **-4% m/m** (sharpest since April 2024)
- Daily fees down **-14% m/m**
- New addresses **-1% m/m**

## On-Chain Holder Behavior
### Medium-term holders SELLING:
- 1-2yr cohort: **-900 bps m/m**
- 2-3yr cohort: **-1250 bps m/m**
- 3-5yr cohort: **-550 bps m/m**

### Long-term holders HOLDING:
- 5-7yr cohort: **+27 bps m/m**
- 7-10yr cohort: **-18 bps m/m**
- >10yr cohort: **+50 bps m/m**

## Institutional Flows
- **DATs (Digital Asset Treasuries):** Added **42k BTC** (+4% m/m) - largest since July 2025
- Total DAT holdings: **1.09m BTC**
- Strategy alone added **29.4k BTC**
- **BTC ETPs:** Holdings declined **-120 bps m/m** to **1.308m BTC**

## Miner Economics
- Breakeven electricity price for S19 XP ASIC:
  - December 2024: **$0.12/kWh**
  - December 2025: **$0.077/kWh**
- Chinese miners in Xinjiang shut down **1.3 GW** capacity
- Estimated **~400k mining machines** shut down
- Up to **10%** of network hash power may be removed

## Historical Hash Rate Analysis (Since 2014)
- When hash rate is shrinking: **65%** of 90-day forward returns positive
- When hash rate is growing: **54%** of 90-day forward returns positive
- When 90-day hash rate growth negative: **77%** of 180-day forward returns positive (avg +72%)
- Outside those periods: **61%** positive (avg +48%)
- Buying during negative hash rate growth historically improved 180-day returns by **+2400 bps**

## Key Takeaways
1. Miner capitulation may signal a bottom (contrarian bullish)
2. "Diamond hands" divergence: Medium-term selling, long-term holding
3. Corporations buying dip while ETPs fade
4. Weak on-chain activity but improving liquidity conditions
5. Reset in speculative leverage points to cautious optimism
