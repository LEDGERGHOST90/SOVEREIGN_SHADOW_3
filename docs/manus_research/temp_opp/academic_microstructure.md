# Academic Research: Cryptocurrency Market Microstructure

## Source: Annals of Operations Research (2024)
## Authors: José Almeida & Tiago Cruz Gonçalves
## Citation: Volume 332, pages 1035–1068
## Published: October 27, 2023
## Metrics: 13k Accesses, 25 Citations

### Study Overview

This systematic literature review analyzed 138 research papers from ABS-listed journals, using bibliographic coupling methodology to map cryptocurrency market microstructure research.

### Key Findings on Market Efficiency

The literature presents heterogeneous conclusions regarding cryptocurrency market microstructure:

**Studies indicating market inefficiency:**
- Akyildirim et al. (2021)
- Grobys et al. (2020)
- Sapkota & Grobys (2021)
- Vidal-Tomás et al. (2019)

**Studies indicating market efficiency:**
- Alvarez-Ramirez & Rodriguez (2021)
- Burggraf & Rudolf (2020)
- Caporale & Plastun (2019)
- Kaiser (2019)
- Lim et al. (2016)

### Market Interconnectedness

**Studies suggesting interconnectedness:**
- Corbet et al. (2018)
- Huynh et al. (2018)
- Shahzad et al. (2021)
- Tiwari et al. (2020)

**Studies suggesting independence:**
- Kostika & Laopodis (2020)
- Sifat et al. (2019)

### Connection to Other Assets

**Studies suggesting connection to traditional assets:**
- Kalyvas et al. (2021)
- Kurka (2019)
- Luu et al. (2020)
- Thampanya et al. (2020)

**Studies suggesting disconnection:**
- Corbet et al. (2018)
- Gil-Alana et al. (2020)

### Research Implications

The study provides:
1. Structured networking and clear information for research outlets
2. Valuable insights for crypto investors to understand market microstructure
3. Information for regulators to effectively regulate cryptocurrencies

### Market Characteristics from Literature

The cryptocurrency market has experienced rapid development and is among the fastest-growing world financial markets. It is considered a very popular investment asset among investors, attracting high attention from media, regulators, institutional and individual investors.

### Academic Relevance

This comprehensive review synthesizes, aggregates, and identifies literature gaps in cryptocurrency research, providing a foundation for future academic work in the field.
