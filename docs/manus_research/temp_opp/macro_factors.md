# Macro Factors Affecting Crypto Markets - December 2025

## Federal Reserve Policy

### Recent FOMC Decision (December 2025)
- Fed voted 9-2-1 to cut Fed Funds rate by 25 basis points
- New range: 3.50-3.75%
- Third consecutive rate cut

### Bitcoin-Fed Correlation
According to TradingView analysis, after every FOMC update, Bitcoin has reacted with a noticeable downside move, underscoring how closely the asset is now tied to monetary policy. However, some analysts note Bitcoin's muted reaction to recent Fed decisions may indicate a maturing market.

### Key Macro Risks

1. **Elevated Inflation:** Persistent inflation concerns limiting Fed's ability to cut aggressively
2. **Liquidity Concerns:** Fed's $40B monthly Treasury purchases creating complex liquidity dynamics
3. **Rate Cut Expectations:** Market uncertainty about pace of future cuts
4. **Trump Administration:** New Fed Chair expected to cut rates "by a lot" according to Trump statements

## Stablecoin Risks (Fed Research)

The Federal Reserve published research on December 17, 2025 examining lessons from the Silicon Valley Bank failure and its impact on stablecoins. Key findings:
- SVB announcement precipitated surge in USDC redemption requests
- USDC lost its peg against the dollar on secondary markets
- Highlights systemic risks in stablecoin ecosystem tied to traditional banking

## Investor Sentiment

Reuters reports that the recent crypto market bust has left some investors more cautious, with the hottest and most-hyped corners of the industry hit hardest. This suggests a shift toward more conservative strategies and risk management.
